The washing machine is leaking water from the bottom. Possible causes include a damaged door seal, a loose drain hose, or a clogged filter.
The refrigerator is not cooling properly. Common issues are dirty condenser coils, a faulty thermostat, or blocked air vents inside the fridge.
The oven does not heat up when turned on. This can be caused by a broken heating element, a faulty temperature sensor, or an issue with the power supply.
The dishwasher leaves dishes dirty. This may be due to a clogged spray arm, low water pressure, or using too little detergent.
The dryer is running but clothes stay wet. Possible reasons include a blocked vent, a broken heating element, or overloading the machine.
